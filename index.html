Online Coding Camp For Kids Aged 7 - 15

Hey! Let’s Code is a week long Summer Camp for children between the ages of 7 and 15 to learn the foundation of coding principles in a fun and collaborative way. We teach our students the basics of how to use tools like “Scratch” during a week long camp from highly trained coaches. Throughout the day we have time for learning as well as fun activities. Hey! Let’s Code is a way for your children to learn a valuable skill and make new friends from the safety of your home.
