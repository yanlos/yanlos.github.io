Our Sessions

We are offering an hourly class for our students in order to accommodate for individual learning patterns. They are 2 weeks long going from Monday to Friday for one hour a day. The cost is 100$ for two weeks.

We also offer all day camps, consisting of 5 hours a day for 1 week. The cost is 250$. Your child will play similar games to the ones we'll be making for inspiration, watch our presentations about the tech world and icons to inspire them, and other activities so its not just coding the whole day to keep our students engaged.
